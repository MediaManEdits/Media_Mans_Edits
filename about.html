ABOUT ME

Sup. I'm MediaMuse (formerly known as MediaMan).

I started creating these edits mainly as a way for me to get off. Having more specific tastes led to there not being a lot of content that I was fond of, so I began to stitch together what I liked to create customized media for myself to consume. Since then, I've branched out and found myself through my own work. Some of my best works are overly emotional.

Been obsessed with video since the days of pirated WMV files and RealPlayer. Now I just do it louder, faster, and weirder.

This site? 100% old-school HTML. I hate JS, frameworks, and all the other things that bloat the modern web. Consider this a sanctuary in a sea of burnt data.

If you're into raw, unfiltered energy in your edits, stick around. Check the MY EDITS tab for the goods.

Questions? Hit up the CONTACT page. Or just lurk. That's cool too.
